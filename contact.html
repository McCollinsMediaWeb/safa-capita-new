Board of directors
Salem Al Mazrouei
Chairman And Board Member
 
Mr Faheem Aziz has over 25 years of experience and an in-depth understanding of Investment Banking, Asset Management, Private Equity, Private Banking and Compliance/ Risk from working in the front, middle and back office. He has worked for some of the leading financial institutions in the world such as Abu Dhabi Investment Authority (ADIA), Credit Suisse, UBS, Swicorp, Hambros, Lehmans and Nomura. He has worked on projects for Deutsche Bank, ABN Amro and RBS amongst others. He was instrumental in raising $1.5bn for a private equity fund that invested in 30 different companies. Whilst at ADIA he was responsible for a team that invested $30bn in world financial markets and set up the Tactical Asset Allocation Unit with an initial investment of $90bn.
Recently he has been the founder of Kinetrix Consulting a pioneer of outsourced Compliance and AML services to Financial institutions regulated by the DFSA. Over the past decade he has been an Authorised DFSA Compliance Officer and MLRO for several DFSA Cat 4, CAT 3c and 3a Firms . Further, he was instrumental in setting the capital markets strategy in Saudi Arabia and was tasked with reviewing the move to T+2 and the inclusion of Saudi in the MSCI index. More recently Mr Aziz has acquired a deep understanding of Cryptocurrencies and blockchain and is currently advising a number of companies in this area. Faheem has an MBA from the University of Oxford Saïd Business School.
